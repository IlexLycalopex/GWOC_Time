Break rules: A 15-minute unpaid break is required for every complete 6-hour block worked. Examples: under 6h = no break required; 6–11h 59m = 15 min; 12–17h 59m = 30 min. Net hours = gross hours minus break taken. Amber flag = insufficient break logged. Red flag = no break logged when one is required. Non-compliant shifts are flagged for manager review — no automatic adjustment is made.
